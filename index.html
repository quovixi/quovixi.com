Product manager by day. Nerd 24/7. Here are some projects I wrote, designed, and (with help from ChatGPT) coded.

LinkedInspo

A random prompt generator for when you can't think what to post on LinkedIn, with example posts. Filter by category: informative, personal, inspiring, or engaging.

RSVPcalc

A simple tool for forecasting attendance at your invite-only event. Work forwards from invites sent, or backwards from places to fill.

rankly.

A web app for ranking up to 10 items by selecting your preference from all possible pairings (my final project for CS50 Introduction to Computer Science).

quonnections

An interactive quiz based on Only Connect's "Connecting Wall" puzzle, where you need to indentify four groups of four related words.
